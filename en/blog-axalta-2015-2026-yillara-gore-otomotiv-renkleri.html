Why this series matters

Axalta is one of the major coatings companies that identifies a global automotive color of the year based on market and design trends.

This series turns the 2015-2026 color history into practical notes for customers, paint matching and color conversations.

2015-2026 color list

The first group includes 2015 Radiant Red, 2016 Brilliant Blue, 2017 Gallant Gray, 2018 StarLite, 2019 Sahara and 2020 Sea Glass.

The recent group includes 2021 ElectroLight, 2022 Royal Magenta, 2023 Techno Blue, 2024 Starry Night, 2025 Evergreen Sprint and 2026 Solar Boost.
