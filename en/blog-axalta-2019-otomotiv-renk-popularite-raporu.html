Axalta 2019 color popularity report: gray rises in Europe

A Set Otomotiv summary of the gray, white, black and silver trends in Axalta 67th Global Automotive Color Popularity Report.

Industry News2026-04-225 min read
Renk TrendiGri TonlariOtomotiv

News summary

Axalta 67th Global Automotive Color Popularity Report notes that white remained globally dominant, with black and gray also ranking strongly.

The report also points to gray becoming the leading automotive color in Europe for the first time, while silver continued to lose ground.

Why it matters for Set Otomotiv customers

European vehicles can have many gray variants. The same model may differ by year, plant, paint code and variant.

Gray color matching should combine the vehicle code, physical sample and spray-out panel rather than relying only on a digital swatch.

Application note

For metallic gray and silver, flake orientation, spray gun settings, coat thickness and clearcoat finish can change the perceived color.
