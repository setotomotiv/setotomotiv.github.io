Axalta 2020 automotive color popularity report: key notes

A practical summary of Axalta 68th Global Automotive Color Popularity Report from a paint and customer preference perspective.

News summary

Axalta 68th Global Automotive Color Popularity Report shows that white remained a leading vehicle color globally, while black and gray also stayed among the top color families.

The report highlights the rise of gray and the decline of silver, reflecting a shift toward more modern and premium neutral tones.

Why it matters for Set Otomotiv customers

White, black and gray remain high-volume color families in repair and refinishing work. Correct code, variant and lighting checks are essential.

Gray color families often include metallic, pearl or effect variations. A color name alone is not enough for reliable matching.

Application note

Neutral colors can look easy, but white and gray variants can differ noticeably because of aging, panel position and effect pigment behavior.
