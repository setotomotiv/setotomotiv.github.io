Axalta 2024 Global Automotive Color: Starry Night

A practical Set Otomotiv note on Starry Night, automotive color trends and paint application sensitivity.

Industry News2026-04-224 min read
AxaltaStarry NightAuto Paint

Color summary

Axalta highlighted Starry Night for 2024. Its color character is: shimmering black with cosmic depth.

Starry Night takes the classic strength of black into a deeper modern finish with blue and silver effects.

What it means for Set Otomotiv

Trend colors like Starry Night are useful references when explaining color character and guiding special shade requests.

These colors should be evaluated together with lighting, panel angle and existing paint aging before local repair, part painting or full repaint decisions.

Application note

Black finishes reveal waves, dust and polishing marks quickly, so preparation and a clean booth are critical.

Effect black colors must be checked from different angles before delivery.
