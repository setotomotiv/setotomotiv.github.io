How to choose primer for industrial paint applications

A short guide to primer selection for metal, machinery, profiles and industrial surfaces.

Industrial Paint2026-04-225 min read
Industrial PaintPrimerMetal Surface

The surface type decides

Primer choice changes depending on whether the surface is steel, galvanized metal, aluminum, sheet metal or old painted surface.

A wrong primer can weaken the whole paint system.

Corrosion requirements

Outdoor exposure, humidity, chemicals and friction increase the need for corrosion resistance.

In those cases the full system should be considered: cleaning, primer, intermediate coat and top coat.

Application conditions

Temperature, humidity, film thickness and drying time affect primer performance.

Mixing ratios and waiting times in technical data sheets should be followed.

This article is for general information. Before application, technical data sheets, physical charts and expert checks should be considered.

Quick Notes

  • Physical verification is the safest method.
  • Technical data sheets should be checked before application.
  • Expert support reduces material and time loss.
