Privacy and Cookie Policy

This site runs statically on GitHub Pages and is designed with a minimal data processing approach.

Data approach

This site does not use non-essential cookies.

User experience settings such as language preference may be stored in localStorage within the browser. This is not used for advertising, profiling or third-party tracking.

Collected data

  • No server-side form data is collected.
  • Analytics and advertising cookies are disabled by default.
  • Language preference can be stored in local browser storage.

External links

  • WhatsApp, Instagram and map links lead to third-party services.
  • The privacy conditions of the relevant platform apply on those links.
  • If analytics are added later, an explicit consent setup should be implemented separately.

GDPR

For personal data rights and controller information, please review the GDPR Notice.
